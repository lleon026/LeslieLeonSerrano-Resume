Social Work

My name is Leslie Leon and I am majoring in sociology because I want to become a social worker for those in need. I come from Watts where the community is not the best. It is a dangerous community and there is a lot of drug use and children in need of help. The reason why I decided to study sociology was because of the community I am from. I want to be able to come back to the community and help out people in need. A lot of children are lost and sometimes their parents are not on the right track and children are suffering and need help.

While growing up, I saw a lot of my friends facing issues and not having a professional to talk to. A lot of children lack from that in the community I was raised. Majoring in sociology will help out other children that are in need of someone to talk to. It could even be a change in the community if there were more professionals helping out.

In that community, not a lot of students get the opportunity to go to college, but I was lucky enough. I do not want to go to college and graduate and not go back and help the community I grew up in. I want to help out others that are in the position I was back then. I want to show others to not give up and that going to college is possible.
